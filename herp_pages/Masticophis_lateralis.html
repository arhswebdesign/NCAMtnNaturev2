Habitat and Range
Found at altitudes between 0–2,250 metres (0–7,382 ft). It is known to use a wide variety of habitat types including the California coast and the foothills, the chaparral of northern Baja, mixed deciduous and pine forests of the Sierra de Juárez, and deserts and canyons of Sierra San Pedro.
