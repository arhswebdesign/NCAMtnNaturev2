Habitat and Range
This snake occupies a wide diversity of habitats, from shrubby coastal dunes to timberline, from shrubby basins and canyons to open mountain forests. It is primarily terrestrial but sometimes climbs into trees or shrubs. When inactive, it occupies mammal burrows, crevices, caves, or similar secluded sites.
