Euphydryas editha

Common name
Edith's Checkerspot
Family
Nymphalidae
Family common name
Brush-footed butterflies
On the wing
E April to L August, peak in May-July, depending on altitude
One generation
Habitat
Sage-steppe hills, subalpine and alpine meadows.
Larval host:
Beardtongue, indian paintbrush, blue-eyed Mary, monkeyflower and others
Nectars on:
Camas, stonecrop, phacelia, pussy paws, pearly everlasting, yellow composites, others. Males hilltop and visit mud.
Abundance
U, C at high elevations
