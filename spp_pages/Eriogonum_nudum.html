Eriogonum nudum

Common name
Naked buckwheat
Family
Polygonaceae
Family common name
Buckwheat family
Blooms
May - September
Habitat and Range
Open areas, especially sand or gravel, in foothill woodland, chaparral, and mixed coniferous forest, below 6300'.
Butterfly associations
Acmon blue uses Naked buckwheat as a larval host
Square-spotted blue uses Naked buckwheat as a larval host
Blue copper uses Naked buckwheat as a larval host
