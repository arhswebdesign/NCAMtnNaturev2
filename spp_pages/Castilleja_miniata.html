Castilleja miniata

Common name
Giant Red Indian Paintbrush
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape family
Blooms
May - September
Habitat and Range
Wetlands, montane meadows,and stream banks in mixed coniferous, upper montane and subalpine forests, 5000' - 11500'.
Differences from C. applegatei
Leaves have straight (non-wavy) margins. Top lip of each individual floret (flower) is yellow with a red margin. Wetter places.
