Pedicularis groenlandica

Common name
Elephant's head
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape family
Blooms
June - September
Habitat and Range
Wetlands and streamsides in mixed coniferous, upper montane and subalpine forests, and the alpine zone, 4000' - 13000'.
Differences from P. attolens
Max. height: 30"., max. leaf length 10", flowers 1/2" wide, elephant's "trunk" is long.
