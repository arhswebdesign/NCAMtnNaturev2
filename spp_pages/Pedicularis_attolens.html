Pedicularis attolens

Common name
Little elephant's head
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape family
Blooms
June - September
Habitat and Range
Wetlands and streamsides in mixed coniferous, upper montane and subalpine forests, and the alpine zone, 4000' - 13000'.
Differences from P. groenlanidica
Max. height: 2 ft., max. leaf length 8", densely hairy inflorescence, flowers 1/3" wide, elephant's "trunk" is short.
