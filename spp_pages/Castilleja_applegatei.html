Castilleja applegatei

Common name
Wavyleaf Paintbrush
Family
Orobanchaceae
Family common name
Broomrape family
Blooms
May - September
Habitat and Range
Open, especially dry areas in mixed coniferous, upper montane and subalpine forests, and sagebrush scrub, below 11900'.
Differences from C. miniata
Leaves have wavy margins. Top lip of each individual floret (flower) is red. Dry soils.
