Erythranthe primuloides

Common name
Primrose monkeyflower
Family
Phrymaceae
Family common name
Lopseed family
Blooms
June - August
Habitat and Range
Wet meadows, seeps, stream banks, and peatlands in mixed coniferous, upper montane and subalpine forests, and occasionally, the alpine zone, 2000' - 11000'.
Differences from E. guttata
Plant up to 5" tall
Leaves in a rosette
Single flower on a thread-like stalk
Trumpet-shaped flower with equal lobes
Flower less than 3/4 in.
