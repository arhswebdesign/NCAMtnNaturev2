Erythranthe guttata

Common name
Seep-spring monkeyflower
Family
Phrymaceae
Family common name
Lopseed family
Blooms
March - August
Habitat and Range
Wetlands in foothill woodland, mixed coniferous and upper montane forests, below 8300'.
Differences from E. primuloides
Plant up to 3' tall
Leaves opposite, up to 3" long
Inflorescence is a raceme of at least 5 flowers
Flowers up to 1½ in.
